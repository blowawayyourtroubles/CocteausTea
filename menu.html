Tea Origins Around the World

Discover the rich cultural heritage and unique flavors of teas from different regions across the globe

China

The birthplace of tea, China offers diverse varieties like delicate green teas, complex oolongs, and aged pu-erh. Regions like Fujian and Yunnan produce teas with centuries of tradition.

Explore Chinese teas →

India

World-renowned for robust Assam and floral Darjeeling teas. India's varied climates produce distinctive flavors, from malty breakfast teas to delicate high-grown varieties.

Japan

Master of green tea, Japan specializes in shaded teas like matcha and gyokuro, with precise steaming techniques that create vibrant, umami-rich flavors.
